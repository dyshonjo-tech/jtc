Our Story

Three decades of
chasing rare light.


Lumière Gems was founded in 1994 by gemologist Élise Vauclaire, whose obsession with extraordinary stones led her from Antwerp's diamond district to the ruby mines of Myanmar and the emerald valleys of Colombia.

Every stone we carry has been personally examined, ethically sourced, and certified by independent gemological laboratories. We don't sell stones we wouldn't keep ourselves.
